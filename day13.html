Day 13 — Values & Direction

30 Days Upstream · Purpose Alignment

Values are not goals.
They are directions you choose to walk in daily.

Why Values Matter

Without values, effort scatters. With values, even small actions move you upstream.

Values vs Goals

Goals are destinations. Values are orientations. You never finish living a value.

  • Goal: write a book → Value: expression
  • Goal: lose weight → Value: vitality
  • Goal: earn money → Value: contribution

Exercise — Clarifying Core Values

  1. Write five values that matter most to you.
  2. Circle the two that feel non-negotiable.
  3. Notice where your daily actions contradict them.

Alignment Creates Energy

Exhaustion often signals misalignment. Energy returns when behavior matches values.

  • Chronic frustration
  • Apathy or resentment
  • Constant distraction

Daily Practice

Choose one action today that expresses a core value, even if it feels small.

Journaling — Reflection

  • Which value guided me today?
  • Where did I resist alignment?
  • What felt energizing?

Orientation Question

“Is this action moving me toward or away from what matters?”
